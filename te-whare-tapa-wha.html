Te Whare Tapa Wha

Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing)

Making sure I get at least 8 hours of sleep.
Getting some movement in each day. (at least short walk to get some fresh air and sun)
Limit caffeine and have healthy food in general.
Go to gym or a run in if I can.

Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing)

Reducing screen time.
Reading something I enjoy.
Making time to relax and unwind.
Doing activities that are fun! e.g dancing
Keeping my space tidy - declutter.

Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing)

To be more present and focus on my life as it is currently.
Focus on my hobbies.
Listening to music.

Taha Whanau (Family Wellbeing)

Keeping in touch with friends and family.
Make time to catch up with people in the weekend.

Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment)

Appreciating the land and nature around me.
Walk along the beach.
